Survey Reminding

Survey reminders are managed automatically in the backend. After 7 unanswered reminders, patient messaging stops and staff are alerted.

Go to Survey Reminder Center
Reminder Centre Guide
  1. Input Appointment: Enter the scheduled date and time for this patient's next visit.
  2. WhatsApp Reminders: Use "Send Now" for manual notifications, or the system will automatically send a reminder 24 hours before the appointment.
  3. Birthday Alerts: On the patient's birthday, an alert will appear on the dashboard. Staff can then send or edit an AI-generated birthday card.
  4. Survey reminders (Work in progress)
