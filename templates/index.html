How to Use Dashboard
  1. Add patient details in Add Patient Data.
  2. Use Patient Data to open a patient record and schedule appointments.
  3. Check Send Birthday Card daily and send cards manually.
  4. Configure alert recipients in Staff Alert Settings.
  5. Use Survey Reminding to run reminder jobs and maintain survey links.
