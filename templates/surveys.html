How to use the CSV upload
  1. In Qualtrics, go to your survey → Data & Analysis → Export & Import → Export Data.
  2. Choose CSV format and download the file.
  3. Make sure the CSV has a column named PID (or whatever you configured above) that contains the patient ID numbers matching your registered patients.
  4. Upload the CSV above. The system will match PIDs to patients and identify who still needs to fill in the survey.
  5. Use Send Reminders Now to immediately send Cantonese WhatsApp messages to all non-completers, or enable Daily Reminders to automate this every morning.
