### How to Use the HealthBot Dashboard

**1. Patient Management**
- Use the **Add Patient Data** section to register new patients.
- **PID** must follow the format `P01` to `P99`.
- **Phone** must be in the format `852xxxxxxxx`.
- You can **Delete** patient data if needed; this will also remove their appointment and survey history.

**2. Survey Reminders**
- The system automatically syncs survey responses from the SFTP folder (`/home/qualtricssftp/uploads`).
- If a patient hasn't filled in a survey, they receive a daily reminder.
- After **7 failed attempts**, the patient stops receiving reminders and an alert is sent to **Staff WhatsApp Numbers**.
- You can set individual survey links in the **Survey Reminding** center.

**3. Appointments**
- Schedule appointments in the **Patient Detail** page.
- Reminders are automatically sent 1 day before the appointment at 09:00 HKT (GMT+8).

**4. Birthday Cards**
- Patients with birthdays today appear in the **Send Birthday Card** section.
- Click **Prepare Card** to generate a personalized message using AI, then review and click **Send**.
